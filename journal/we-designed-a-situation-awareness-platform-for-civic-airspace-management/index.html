Aviation
For Passur Aerospace
  • Data visualization
  • User centred design

Reducing waste with business intelligence

The US civil aviation industry wastes $31bn dollars per year on inefficiencies such as delays, circling planes and refunds for late arrivals. Passur’s software helps airlines, airport executives and government officials manage their fleets, sites and networks better by gathering data to feed predictive models.

Passur aviation asked us to help them better serve their users by surfacing the predictive intelligence that lies at the heart of their platform. We worked with them to design user-focused software that sped up and simplified the delivery of the rich information their platform proves to people in an understandable and convenient way.
