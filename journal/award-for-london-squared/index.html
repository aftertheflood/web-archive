Award for London Squared

3 Dec 2015

Our mapping project London Squared was awarded last night in the Information is Beautiful Awards, sponsored by Kantar.

It was a competitive field and we are all happy that this work continues to be recognised. 

The project, a collaboration with Future Cities Catapult equalises London borough areas to better display data.

Since the collaboration, we created a free editor tool – used by many organisations including the GLA and London Fire Brigade.

We continue to use this system in client work too and plan to expand the editor in the new year.

Well done to the other winners too.
